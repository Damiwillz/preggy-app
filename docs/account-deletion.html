Preggy Account Deletion Request

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Preggy users can request deletion of their account and associated app data.

How to request account deletion

Email:

dammywillz@gmail.com

Use this subject line:

Preggy Account Deletion Request

In your message, include the email address linked to your Preggy account.

What will be deleted

When your request is verified, Preggy will delete or anonymize account data associated with your account, including:

  • Profile data
  • Pregnancy profile details
  • Appointment records
  • Medication and supplement records
  • Symptom logs
  • Privacy and app settings
  • Profile avatar, where applicable
  • AI chat history, where applicable

Verification

We may ask you to confirm ownership of the account before deletion.

Retention

Some information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or operational reasons.

Contact

For account deletion questions, contact:

dammywillz@gmail.com
