# Privacy Policy for Preggy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Preggy is a pregnancy wellness and organization app created to help users track pregnancy progress, symptoms, appointments, medication reminders, privacy settings, and daily guidance.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Preggy collects, how it is used, how it is protected, and how users can request access, export, or deletion of their data.

## Information we collect

Preggy may collect and store the following information when you use the app:

- Account information, such as email address and profile name
- Pregnancy profile details, such as baby nickname, due date, pregnancy week, and pregnancy day
- Appointment details, such as appointment title, doctor, clinic, date, time, status, and notes
- Medication and supplement details, such as medication name, dosage, frequency, notes, and reminder preferences
- Symptom logs, such as mood, symptoms, intensity, notes, and date logged
- Privacy and app settings, such as theme mode, accent color, biometric lock preference, AI chat history preference, and notification preferences
- Profile avatar image, if you choose to upload one
- AI chat messages, if you use the Preggy AI feature and enable or allow chat history

## How we use your information

Preggy uses your information to provide app functionality, including:

- Creating and managing your account
- Showing your pregnancy timeline and weekly growth
- Saving your symptoms, appointments, and medications
- Sending medication and appointment reminders
- Displaying your profile and avatar
- Saving your privacy, appearance, and notification settings
- Providing AI powered wellness support when you use Preggy AI
- Allowing you to export or manage your data

Preggy is a wellness and organization companion. It is not a medical device and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always contact your doctor, midwife, or emergency services for urgent medical concerns.

## How your information is stored

Preggy uses Supabase to store account and app data securely. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections. Preggy uses access controls so users can only access their own private account data.

Profile avatar images may be stored in Supabase Storage. Avatar images may be publicly readable through a generated image URL so they can be displayed in your profile.

## AI chat

Preggy AI may send your message to an AI service provider so a response can be generated. Do not enter emergency medical information or highly sensitive information into Preggy AI.

Preggy AI is for general wellness support and organization. It should not be used for diagnosis, treatment, or emergency decisions.

## Notifications

Preggy may request notification permission to send reminders for medications, supplements, and appointments. Notifications are only used for app reminder functionality.

You can disable notifications in your device settings.

## Data sharing

Preggy does not sell personal or sensitive user data.

Preggy may share data with service providers only as needed to run app functionality, such as:

- Supabase, for authentication, database, and storage
- Expo, for app services and notifications
- AI service providers, only when you use Preggy AI

Preggy may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect users, the app, or others.

## Data retention

Preggy keeps your account data while your account is active.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting dammywillz@gmail.com. When deletion is completed, Preggy will delete or anonymize account data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or operational reasons.

## Data export

Preggy includes a data export option inside the app. You can use this feature to download or review your stored app data.

## Account deletion

You can request account deletion by emailing dammywillz@gmail.com with the subject line:

Preggy Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address linked to your Preggy account. We may ask you to confirm ownership of the account before deleting data.

## Children

Preggy is not intended for children. Users should only use Preggy if they are old enough to create an account and provide consent under the laws that apply to them.

## Security

Preggy uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data. No system is completely secure, so users should keep their login details private and contact us if they believe their account has been accessed without permission.

## Changes to this policy

Preggy may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date.
